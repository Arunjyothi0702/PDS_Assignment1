Height: Continuous variable measured in inches representing the height of the participants.
Weight: Continuous variable measured in pounds representing the weight of the participants.
Age: Continuous variable measured in years representing the age of the participants.
Grip strength: Continuous variable measured in kilograms representing the grip strength of the participants.
Frailty: Categorical variable indicating the presence or absence of frailty symptoms. It has two categories: "Y" for presence and "N" for absence.
