Height: The participants' height is a continuous variable expressed in inches.
Weight: The continuous variable, expressed in pounds, denotes the participants' weight.
Age: A continuous variable that represents the participants' ages in years.

Grip strength: A continuous variable that reflects the individuals' grip strength and is measured in kilograms.
The presence or absence of frailty symptoms is indicated by the categorical variable "fragilty." There are two categories: "Y" denotes presence and "N" indicates absence.
